About the Garden

We embeded the smart microcontroller into the garden and now it is self reliant!

Raspberry Pi

intelligently controls the garden while simultaneously sending data to store in Firebase, allowing the web application to see the update in real time

Multiple Functions

Treshhold is pre-calculated to suit your plant condition, so the amount of water and temperature or even light setting of your garden are adjusted perfectly

Visualizations

Graph and data are displayed on the web so you don't ever have to worry about your garden

Remote Control

Our web application service allows you to make a change in garden environmental setting remotely
